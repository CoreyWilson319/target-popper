Target Popper!

Target Popper, is a game where the player should aim to score above 100 points in a single round to win, after reaching that goal the player can strive to beat their score during that session. Random targets will appear during the interval of the timer and bonuses are given for reaching certain milestones and allow the player to increase the time remaining to an extent. The player may click the start button at anytime while the game is running to restart the timer and try again. Can you beat the high score?
